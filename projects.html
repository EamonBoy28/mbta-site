Red Line Transformation

"As part of the Capital Transformation Program, the Red Line Transformation (RLT) is improving the quality of service for the T’s most popular line."

Involves:

  • Red Line Signal Upgrades (Nov 2018-Summer 2023, 48% complete)
  • 252 New Red Line Vehicles (2014-2024, 12/252 cars delivered, 8/252 in service)
  • Red Line Test Track (complete)
  • Cabot Yard and Maintenance Facility Upgrades (Aug 2018-Summer 2023, 32% complete)
  • Codman Yard Expansion and Improvements (unknown completion)
  • Alewife Crossover Improvements (Jul 2019-Fall 2022, unknown completion)

Orange Line Transformation

"As part of the Capital Transformation Program, the Orange Line Transformation (OLT) is upgrading the 120-year-old rapid transit line serving the communities of Malden, Medford, Somerville, and Boston."

Involves:

  • Orange Line Signal Upgrades (Nov 2018-Fall 2023, unknown completion)
  • 152 New Orange Line Vehicles (2014-2023, unknown/152 cars delivered, unknown/152 in service)
  • Wellington Yard and Vehicle Maintenance Facility Updates
  • Orange Line Traction Power Substation Upgrades (Spring 2015-Fall 2025, 78% complete)
  • Test Track Rehabilitation (complete)
  • Storage Track Expansion (complete)

Green Line Transformation

"As part of the Capital Transformation Program, the Green Line Transformation (GLT) is upgrading service on the T’s most popular light rail line."

Involves:

  • Green Line Train Protection System (Jan 2020-Jun 2025, unknown completion)
  • Hynes Station Accessibility Improvements (Spring 2020-Fall 2025, unknown completion)
  • Solar Powered E-Ink Signs (Spring 2022-Early 2024, 4 stops complete)
  • D Branch Station Accessibility Improvements (Design Feb 2020-Fall 2022, 30% completed, Brookline Hills complete)
  • Green Line Track Upgrades 2022 (complete)
  • Lechmere Viaduct Rehabilitation (complete)
  • D Branch Track and Signal Replacement (complete)
  • B Branch Station Consolidation (complete)
  • 24 New Green Line Vehicles (complete)
  • Track and Intersection Upgrades (complete)
  • Fenway Portal Flood Protection (complete)
  • Green Line Extension (complete)
